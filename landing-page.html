Rate That Song: A new way to socialize over music.

Brief Description:

Music is a great way to get to know people, as people who listen to the same music tend to get along. With Rate That Song, we are providing a new way to discuss and enjoy music online. Rate, rank and make comments about your favorite songs, all on this one site!

List of Planned Features:

  • Rate music and share your favorite songs.
  • Recieve music recommendations based on your favorite songs and people who like the same song.
  • Upload and share your own music to others

Membership Options:

  • Free Membership Plan: Free to sign up. Includes all the base features of the app.
  • Premium Plan: $5.99 per month. Includes no advertisements, increased importance to your ratings, and all the features of the base plan.
  • Extra Premium Plan: $9.99 per month or $99.99 per year. Includes all the features of the other two plans, as well as the increased ability to promote your own music.
